Q: Where can I find information on collecting sales tax in Alaska?
A: Businesses with physical locations in Alaska should consult with the Alaska Department of Revenue - Tax Division.

Q: What is economic nexus in Alaska?
A: Alaska's economic nexus law requires remote sellers and marketplace facilitators to register with the commission and collect and remit sales tax if they have $100,000 or more in annual gross receipts from sales, or 200 or more sales annually into the state.

Q: Does Alaska have a state-wide sales tax?
A: No, Alaska does not have a state-wide sales tax. However, cities and boroughs in Alaska can require a local sales tax.

Q: How can I file and pay sales tax in Alaska?
A: You can file online at the Alaska Remote Seller Sales Tax Commission and remit your payment through their online system.
